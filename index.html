OVERGROWN

Vertun
CO-OP Puzzle, Platformer (MAX 2 PLAYERS)

The human society was thriving, the future looked very bright. The growing problem with the greenhouse effect had been resolved. Humans had found a way to live in complete harmony with nature without destroying it as they grew.

Earth's savior a new kind of energy that could be implanted in everything and it didn't give out any harmful gases. It even affected plants chemistry and made them grow a lot faster. But the future society destroyed itself with a gigantic explosion when they tried to go too far with this new eco friendly energy.

Trying to turn the massive energy into a even cleaner source. Most of the humans were wiped out, the ones that survived had changed drastically. For some reason, all their body hair disappear and their skin turned skeleton white. Their eyes changed to entirely black. Humans were no longer what they once were. Everything grand that had been built crumbled almost nothing of the humans' grandness was left completely standing. Hundreds and hundreds of years passed, leaving nature time to grow, and grow. It's now natures time to shine, and humans time to adapt.

This leads us to you two, two lonely survivors. Having nothing but your own strengths and weaknesses. Will you survive, in the ruins of your ancestors? Will you be able to pass through the ruins and their challenges? To find a place which you will be able to call home?
